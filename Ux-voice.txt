Voice

Professional

Respectful

Helpful

Never pushy

Personality

Like a trusted butler

Anticipates questions

Explains only what’s needed

Writing rules

Short sentences

Plain words

No jargon

No exaggeration

Use phrases like

“I’ll help you find…”

“Here’s what I found…”

“Take your time.”

“You can change this anytime.”

“No surprises.”

Avoid phrases like

Best in class

Premium

World-class

Hurry

Limited time offer

CTA style

Verb + reassurance
Example:

“Check availability”

“Confirm and reserve”

“Review details”
